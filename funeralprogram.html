Obituary

I, Mandla Nicolas Dlamini,
was born on September 16, 1951, in Evaton. I attended Maqhere Primary School and completed my secondary education at Wilberforce High School.

I was a hardworking and dedicated individual, always striving to achieve my goals.

My career began at Babcock and later at Air Product. Eventually, I ventured into entrepreneurship, starting my own business as a contractor building houses. Later, I founded another business, Mindlosi and Sons Sorghum Distributos.

I married my wonderful wife, Khombisile Dlamini, My MaDlamini.

I am survived by my four children, Seipati, Meki, Teddy, and Sbu, and my five grandchildren. I also leave behind my older brother, Sipho, and my three beloved sisters, Thoko, Dolly, and Ntombifuthi.

To my family, I love you all. Take care of each other and always treat each other with love and respect.
